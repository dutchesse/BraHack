HOW CAN I START HACKING?

Get creative ... that is all there is to it :)

Grab your pencils, scissors, sewing machine, computer tablet, knitting needles ... whatever your preferred creative tool, use it to let your voice be heard.

 

If you already have an idea you want to work on ... enter the workshop by clicking on the button below. If you want to know what issues other women are struggling with, have a look at the Issues page.

 

There are three categories to choose from; fashion Bras, Sports Bras and Fantasy Bras. You can submit as many designs as you like from all categories.

BRA HACK WORKSHOP

The workshop is where you can submit your design hacks,

vote for other submitted hacks you think are a great idea,  or

help improve a hack idea that has great potential but is not quite ready for prototyping just yet.

 

Voting for your favorite bra hacks helps us decide which hacks get tested and turned into

a real life prototype for our volunteers to try them on.
