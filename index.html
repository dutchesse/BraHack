What the Heck is a bra hack?

ONCE UP ON A TIME ... Actually ... plenty of times really and usually during a bra shopping trip, have I wondered why a bra can be such a thing of beauty and at the same time be the subject of one of life's greatest aggravations.

 

Ever since the first patented bra in 1744, the bra gifted us with freedom from the corset, but since the invention of the underwire bra innovation has come to a halt. With todays 3D printers, silicones and mold-able plastics, I am convinced there must be a better & more comfortable way to give the girls support.

 

But why in todays day and age of silicons,  polyolefin gels, body scanning and 3D printing must we still put up with closure hooks, slipping sliders and poky under-wire which are often the guilty culprits of our aggravation with our bras. I can't help but wonder if there is a better way to build these contraptions.

JOIN THE WORLD BRA HACK

My personal theory is that the traditionally male dominated undergarment industry has deemed the bra simply not worthy of improvement and has stuck with the good ole 'if it ain't broke don't fix it' attitude, they keep relying on the same ancient bits and bobs that have supplied the bra industry for many many years.

 

Well this rogue rebel wants to see if she can disrupt that attitude and harness the brain power of all the women in the world willing to participate and share their infinite goddess wisdom and knowledge in the Global Bra Hack.

 

Is there such a thing as the perfect bra? As we are all of different size, shape and proportion, chances are likely there is no single bra that fits all.  But lets see if we collaboratively can re-engineer old standards and propel the bra into the 20th century.
