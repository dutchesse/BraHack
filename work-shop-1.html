WHY HACK A BRA?

Ever since the first patented bra in 1744, the bra gifted us with freedom from the corset and is has undergone many a changes. Ever since the invention of the under wire bra innovation has come to a halt. With todays 3D printers, silicones and mold-able plastics, I am convinced there must be a better & more comfortable way to give the girls support.

 

Put on your thinking cap and get creative ... that is all there is to it :)

Grab your pencils, scissors, sewing machine, computer tablet, knitting needles ... whatever your preferred creative tool, use it to let your voice be heard.

 

If you already have an idea you want to work on ... enter the workshop by clicking on the button below. If you want to know what issues other women are struggling with, have a look at the Issues page.

 

You can choose from three categories; fashion Bras, Sports Bras and Fantasy Bras. Feel free to submit as many designs as you like from any or all categories.
