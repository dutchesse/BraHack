SUBMIT A BRA ISSUE

Voice your issue with a current bra design element. Besides common issues such as under wire poking through and bra hooks getting mangled in the laundry, other annoying issues might be that the bra are straps not wide enough or those little sliders in the back won't stay in place. Weather it is a well known or lesser talked about issue, report any issues that you think re-engineering so that fellow hackers can chime in and start coming up with solutions worth prototyping.
